Season-1
Episode 1

Set in the city of Kota, RVCJ Originals "Khatta Meetha" is a collection of some relatable tales of the Sharma Siblings. The show revolves around siblings, Urvi and Rajul who are preparing for JEE (IIT) and NEET (Medical) exams respectively. Their habit of fighting over trivial issues puts their parents in a problematic position all the time.
